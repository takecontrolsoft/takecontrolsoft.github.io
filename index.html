About Us

Who We Are

Take Control - Software and Infrastructure EOOD develops software solutions and integrations that automate everyday administrative and operational processes.

At the core of our philosophy is the understanding that modern businesses use multiple systems, platforms, and services. No single software product can, or needs to, cover every function. Efficiency comes from connecting existing solutions in the right way.

Our role is to build those connections.

By developing integrations, automated workflows, extensions, and specialized tools, we reduce manual data transfer between systems, minimize error risk, and save time on routine tasks.

We believe that sometimes one well-built small solution can save dozens of hours per month and significantly improve control over business operations.

Our Approach

Efficiency through intelligent connections

We connect systems instead of replacing them
We automate specific processes instead of making work more complex
We create solutions aligned with real business practice
We improve efficiency through intelligent integrations

To reduce administrative burden in business through practical and sustainable automation.
