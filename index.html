About our mission

Take control - software and infrastructure

The main goal of the company is to improve the syncing photos and videos between the phones and the server. Talking to our partners we realized that this is the minimum we can do for improving the daily work for people working not only with photos, but also with any kind of data. We all are working every day with files, numbers and data from different software products. We as a company believe that the software products the users use help them a lot, but in most of the cases they need integrations with external products, modules or services. This is because there is no a software product that can provide all the features they need to do their job. And it is not need to. It is always cheeper to use already existing products instead of designing one big product that cover all our needs. Using different products gives us independency. The issue is that the users become confused. They loose time to switch from one system to another, they loose time to copy and transfer data between the systems. Sometime adding one small piece to the puzzle is enough to make their lives easier and to reduce the mistakes. One small integration, one small plugin, extension, service, or one common portal for all the apps they are using. This is the point where our company can help. We are currently working on some improvements for the accounting agencies, and we can see how really small things can save a dozen hours. We are going to announce our products as soon as they are released. And we are sure they can be helpful for many others. Feel free to share with us your experience with the software products you use and we can think together what can be improved, to search what you can use - some of our or external solutions. We are open for discussions. You can contact us at site@takecontrolsoft.eu>.
