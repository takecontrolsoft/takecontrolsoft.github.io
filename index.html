About our mission

The main goal of the company "Take Control - Software and Infrastructure EOOD" is to improve the daily workflow of the companies, homes or individuals. We develop software products, integrations, automated build and processes, web extensions, mobile applications and web sites, we build cloud infrastructures.
Founding the company was based on the idea for developing an automatic photo synchronization from the phones to a home server.
Later on, working with our partners, we realized that this is the minimum we can do for improving the daily work for people working not only with photos, but also with any kind of data. We all work with files, numbers and data every day. We as a company believe that the software products help a lot to the users, but in most of the cases they need integrations with external products, modules or services. This is because there is no software product that can provide all the features they need to do their job. And it is not needed. It is always cheaper to use already existing products instead of designing one big product that covers all our needs. Using different products gives us independence. The issue is that the users become confused. They lose time to switch from one system to another, they lose time to copy and transfer data between the systems. Sometimes adding one small piece to the puzzle is enough to make their lives easier and to reduce the mistakes. The right integration, plugin, extension, service, or one even one common portal for all the apps they use, will reduce the time for making their jobs done. This is the point where our company can help. We are currently working on some improvements for accounting agencies, and we can see how really small pieces of code can save a dozen hours for daily routine tasks.
We will announce our products as soon as they are released. And we are sure they can be helpful for many others. You can subscribe for the announcements using the button at the end of this page.

Daily use cases

Here are some examples of everyday work processes that could be improved.

Don't use sticky notes

During the daily workflow the tasks appear one after another. If you are still using sticky notes this means that you struggle to find and open the correct application where the task should be added. Or maybe you are missing the correct software at all.

Reduce data input

It is a nightmare when you have to enter the same information to different software systems. It is because you are missing integration between them. They all do perfect job, but it takes from your time to synchronize the data between them.

Organize your apps

In the world of cloud and web applications, you spend most of the days working with the browsers. What if you have one page accessible from all your devices where you can organize the web and desktop applications together.

Sync photos at home

Taking photos and videos every day with your phone, makes it difficult to manage them. The device sync should do it for you to your local home server. All the family members can sync photos to the same place. They will be automatically analyzed and organized.
